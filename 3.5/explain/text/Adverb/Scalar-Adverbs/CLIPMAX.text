	(SCALAR ADVERB)

	CLIPMAX and  CLIPMIN are the maximum and minimum y-values that
	will be found in Array (0) after it has been CLIPped.

	CLIPMAX and CLIPMIN are used by : CLIP

	No verbs change CLIPMAX and CLIPMIN.

	The initial values of CLIPMAX and CLIPMIN are 9999.000 and
 	-9999.000, respectively.

	EXAMPLE :

	To chop a spike down to noise level, specify :

		CLIPMAX = 0.5
		CLIPMIN = -0.5
		CLIP
