	(REGULAR VERB)

	EMPTY will delete all entries from the internal ASTACK array
	and reset the stack counter, ACOUNT, to zero.

	No adverbs are used by EMPTY.

	ADVERBS SET:

	   ACOUNT       The stack counter containing the # of scans in
			the stack.  The initial value is 0.

	EXAMPLE:

	You have just stacked a group of scans and now wish to clear
	ASTACK.  Type:

		EMPTY
